About Us

Cardvo founded in 2022, by Zuri was developed by team proj cc gen/128. Our purpose is to serve you an array of fancy credit card designs, that you can view, edit, download and share with friends on social media platforms. There is an opportunity for you to create your own design based on your preference, for are no limits to your creativity on Cardvo.
Cardvo has about Fifty thousand (50,000) active users around the world, with over two hundred and twenty (220) card designs available in about seventy four (74) countries. If you need to know more or need assistance you can send us a mail via the contact us page. Little things matter to your happiness; unleash your creativity with Cardvo.
